FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers

The things people usually ask.

Is it free?

Yes. Day Tracker is completely free. No subscriptions, no hidden costs, no premium tiers. Just download and start tracking.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely. Day Tracker is built offline-first. Everything works without an internet connection — tasks, goals, alarms, streaks. Your data stays on your device.

Do alarms work when the phone is locked?

Yes. Alarms ring full-screen even when your phone is locked. We built the alarm system to be reliable — so you never miss what matters.

Do I need an account?

Not at all. Day Tracker requires no account, no sign-up, no email. Just download and start using it. Your data stays on your device.

Can I use goals and tasks together?

Yes. Tasks and goals work hand in hand. Break your goals into daily tasks, track progress on both, and see how small steps lead to big achievements.

Can I reschedule alarms?

Yes. With one tap, you can snooze or reschedule any alarm. Your routine stays flexible without falling apart.
